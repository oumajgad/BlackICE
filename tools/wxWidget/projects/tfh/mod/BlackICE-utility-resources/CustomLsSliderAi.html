On this page you can customize the automatic leadership slider logic.
If you want to use it you will have to toggle it here and
activate the "Slider Ai" mode in the technology window.
If you make any change you need to press the "set values" button.

Lower & Upper

  • the values you enter are the resource amounts, not the LS allocation
  • when below the lower threshold the AI will assign LS to increase the amounts
  • it will produce until the upper threshold is reached
  • the amount of LS assigned is determined by the AI and cannot be limited

Extra options

  • officers (NCOs) can be produced for 10 more days to create a buffer of ~500 officers

Notes

  • officers can not have a ratio higher than 110% entered due to engine limitations
  • diplo will take active influences into account when producing new diplo points
  • Order of allocation is: 1.Officers, 2.Spies, 3.Diplo, 4.Research
  • WARNING turning on slider AI will make the game not automatically continue researching techs once a level is finished
